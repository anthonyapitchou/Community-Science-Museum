Accessibility

Our museum is committed to providing an inclusive experience for all visitors. We offer wheelchair access, sensory-friendly exhibits, and assistive listening devices. Please contact us in advance for any specific accommodations you may require.

Restaurant & Cafe

Enjoy a variety of delicious meals and snacks at our on-site restaurant and cafe. We offer a range of options to suit all dietary needs, from light bites to hearty meals. Take a break and refuel during your visit.
